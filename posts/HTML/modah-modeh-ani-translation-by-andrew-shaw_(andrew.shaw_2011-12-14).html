מוֹדָה|מוֹדֶה אֲנִי לְפָנֶיךָ רוּחַ חַי וְקַיָּם שֶׁהֶחֱזַרְתָּ בִּי נִשְׁמָתִי בְּחֶמְלָה רַבָּה אֱמוּנָתֶךָ‏׃
Thankful am I in your Presence, Spirit who lives and endures, for You've returned to me my soul with compassion. Abundant is your faith!
Modeh Ani first appeared as an addendum in Seder ha-Yom by Rav Moshe Ibn Machir of Tzfat, published in 1599. Read more on Modeh Ani at Hardcore Mesorah.
