DOWNLOAD: PDF | ODT A version of the Pesaḥ Haggadah with full cantillation. Also includes several additional optional passages from the editor for those who prefer a more egalitarian rite, as well as a few instructions. This text is meant to be used for reading along with a more traditional haggadah with fuller instructions and discussion prompts, rather than as a replacement.
