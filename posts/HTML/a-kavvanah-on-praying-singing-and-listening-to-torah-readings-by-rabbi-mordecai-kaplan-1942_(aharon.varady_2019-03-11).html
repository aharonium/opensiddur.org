Title: A Kavvanah on Praying, Singing, and Listening to Torah Readings, by Rabbi Mordecai Kaplan (1942)
Author: aharon.varady
Link: http://opensiddur.org/?p=24168

Source (Hebrew)Translation (English)
Let every prayer we recite, every song we sing, every teaching we listen to set the current of Israel's life coursing through our whole being, challenge us to test the ever living truth of what Israel has learned concerning man's task on earth, and reveal to us the God who always stands at the door of our heart waiting as it were to be admitted.
In this spirit let us pray: "May the words of my mouth and the meditations of my heart be acceptable to Thee O God, my strength and my redeemer.‚Äù[foot]Psalms 19.15[/foot]
