I was in a Beirut foxhole, 
1983, 
religious war, 
but U.S. foxholes were unique: 
interfaith. 
Side by side, 
some sought You 
through different religious faith traditions; 
others––not religious––
found faith and common cause in freedom's call, 
in freedom's holy light.
