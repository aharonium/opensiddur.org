We thank Thee,
Lord,
for the diseases
and the suffering
which keep us
from unwarranted pride
and unseeing selfishness,
the torments of body and mind
which make us ever conscious
of our need for each other's help
and our aspirations
toward Thee.

also,
for endowing us
with the wisdom
and the skill
to face our problems
with courage and confidence,
knowing
that Thou art with us,
when we labor
to heal and help
our fellow men.
</div></td></tr>

Be Thou

whose dedicated hands
will make
of these bricks,
