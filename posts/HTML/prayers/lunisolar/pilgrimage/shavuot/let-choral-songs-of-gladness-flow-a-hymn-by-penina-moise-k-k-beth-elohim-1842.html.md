"Let choral songs of gladness flow," by Penina Moïse, was published in 1842, and appears under the subject "Revelation" as Hymn 16 in <em><a href="/?p=39305">Hymns Written for the Service of the Hebrew Congregation Beth Elohim, South Carolina</a></em> (Penina Moïse et al., Ḳ.Ḳ.
