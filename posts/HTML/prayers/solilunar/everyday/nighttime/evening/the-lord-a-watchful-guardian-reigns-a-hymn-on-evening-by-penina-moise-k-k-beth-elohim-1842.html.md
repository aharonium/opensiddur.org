"The Lord a watchful guardian reigns" by Penina Moïse, published in 1842, appears under the subject "Evening" as Hymn 49 in <em><a href="/?p=39305">Hymns Written for the Service of the Hebrew Congregation Beth Elohim, South Carolina</a></em> (Penina Moïse et al., Ḳ.Ḳ. Beth Elohim, 1842), pp. 52-53. In the one copy of this first edition we know to exist, pages 53 through 56 are missing, and so the remaining lines of the fourth stanza and the entirety of the fifth were recovered from the second edition, <em><a href="/?p=39305">Hymns Written for the Use of Hebrew Congregations</a></em> (Penina Moïse et al., Ḳ.Ḳ. Beth Elohim, 1856), p. 140, where it appears as Hymn 143. Punctuation and capitalization for these lines are provided as found under the conventions of the first edition. --Aharon Varady
