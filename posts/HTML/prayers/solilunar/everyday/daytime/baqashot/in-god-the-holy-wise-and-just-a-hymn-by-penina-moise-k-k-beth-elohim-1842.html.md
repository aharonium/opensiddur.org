"In God, the holy, wise, and just," by Penina Moïse, was published in 1842, and appears under the subject of Omniscience as Hymn 6 in <em><a href="/?p=39305">Hymns Written for the Service of the Hebrew Congregation Beth Elohim, South Carolina</a></em> (Penina Moïse et al., Ḳ.Ḳ. Beth Elohim, 1842), pp. 11-12. --Aharon Varady
