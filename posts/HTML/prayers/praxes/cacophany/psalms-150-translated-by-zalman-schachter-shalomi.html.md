הַ֥לְלוּ יָ֨הּ ׀ הַֽלְלוּ־אֵ֥ל בְּקָדְשׁ֑וֹ 
הַֽ֝לְל֗וּהוּ בִּרְקִ֥יעַ עֻזּֽוֹ׃

הַֽ֭לְלוּהוּ בְּתֵ֣קַע שׁוֹפָ֑ר 
הַֽ֝לְל֗וּהוּ בְּנֵ֣בֶל וְכִנּֽוֹר׃
הַֽ֭לְלוּהוּ בְתֹ֣ף וּמָח֑וֹל 
הַֽ֝לְל֗וּהוּ בְּמִנִּ֥ים וְעוּגָֽב׃
</span></div></td>
 
<td style="vertical-align:top;">
<div class="english" lang="en">
<strong>Hallelu-Yah!</strong> Attune to God in holiness: 
be in awe of God who is mighty in the heavens.
mark God for His potent acts: 
praise God for Her generosity.
Salute God with trumpet sound: 
hail God with the strings and harp.
Worship God with drum and dance: 
Intone to God with organ and flute.
</div></td></tr>

כֹּ֣ל הַ֭נְּשָׁמָה תְּהַלֵּ֥ל יָ֗הּ 
הַֽלְלוּ־יָֽהּ׃
</span></div></td>

praise God with cymbals that ring out.
Let all who breathe chant Yah.
<strong>Hallelu-Yah.</strong>
