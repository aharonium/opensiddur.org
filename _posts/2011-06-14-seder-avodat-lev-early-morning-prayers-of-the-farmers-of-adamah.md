Blessed are you, YHVH our g!d, cosmic majesty, who gives understanding to the rooster to distinguish between day and night. 
Blessed are you, Yah our g!d, spirit of the world, who gives sight to the blind. 
Blessed are you, YHVH our g!d, king of the world, who created me in the divine image.
